FAQ

Common questions

Dummy answers — update with your real policies and turnaround times.

What software do you use?

We primarily work in Tekla Structures, Autodesk Revit, Advance Steel, AutoCAD, and Navisworks. We can adapt to your preferred platform and standards.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround depends on project size and complexity. Small packages may be delivered in 1–2 weeks; larger industrial projects are scheduled in agreed milestones.

Do you work with our fabrication standards?

Yes. We adopt your connection details, naming conventions, title blocks, and QA checklists as part of every engagement.

Can you provide resources on a monthly basis?

Yes. Our resource leasing service provides dedicated detailers or modelers embedded in your team for ongoing or peak workload support.
